Common Questions

Straightforward answers — no runaround.

Do you buy cars that are financed or leased?

Yes. We buy financed and leased vehicles regularly. Just let us know your lender when you submit the form and we'll handle the payoff process.

What condition do you buy cars in?

Any condition — running or not, minor wear, major damage, high mileage. We've seen it all with 15+ years in the business. Submit your car and we'll give you an honest offer.

How fast will I get an offer?

We call you the same day in most cases. Once you accept, we move fast — usually wrapping everything up within 24–48 hours.

Are there any fees or commissions?

None. Zero dealer fees, zero commissions. The number we offer you is the number you get paid.

What area do you serve?

We're based in the Inland Empire and serve the surrounding Southern California area. Not sure if we cover your location? Call us and we'll figure it out.

What do I need to sell my car?

Your title (or loan payoff info if financed), a valid ID, and the keys. That's it. We handle the paperwork.
